PSTAR Prep

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

PSTAR Prep (“the app”) is an independent study aid for Canada’s PSTAR aviation regulations examination.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

Data stored on your device

Your study progress, quiz scores, bookmarks, flashcard “mastered” marks, and your acceptance of the in-app disclaimer are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device. Deleting the app removes it.

Network use

The only network requests the app can make are when you tap a link to open the official Transport Canada source document, the privacy policy, or a contact email — each opens in your browser or mail app.

Children’s privacy

The app is a general-audience educational tool and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

Any changes will be reflected in the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy: wonmor@gmail.com

PSTAR Prep is an independent study aid and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Transport Canada. Study material reproduced from Transport Canada TP 11919E (Dec 2022).
